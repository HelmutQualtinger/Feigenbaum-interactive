Bifurcation Diagram of the Logistic Map

Interactive Visualization of Feigenbaum's Period-Doubling Route to Chaos

The logistic map is defined by the recurrence relation:

Xn+1 = r · Xn · (1 − Xn)

where r is a parameter controlling the system's behavior. As r increases from 0 to 4, the system exhibits a period-doubling cascade, ultimately leading to chaotic dynamics.
